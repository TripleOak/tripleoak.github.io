About Us

At Triple Oak we provide seed funding and support for startups and small companies with big dreams.
With our likeminded partners, we believe in innovation and helping companies develop their ideas. Whether it’s support in technology, legal or management. We are there for you.
Our fields of interest are artificial intelligence, big data, cyber security, biotech, fintech and agriculture, but we are not limited to the interests stated above. You are welcome to apply to our funding!

For Investors

We are always looking for likeminded partners and companies who want to invest in innovation with us.

For Companies

If you are not afraid to pitch your innovative idea or a brand-new startup, let’s talk! We are looking forward to your story.
